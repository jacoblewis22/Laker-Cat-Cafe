About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Laker Cat Lounge, where cats and coffee collide for one purrfect establishment - run by Grand Valley State University (GVSU) students for GVSU students.

We are a student-run organization looking to give cat loving GVSU students of all majors a place to come and hang out, study, or just come be surrounded by cats. We also have a selection of drinks and food, all themed after our furry friends.

Locations

We have a couple locations right next to Grand Valley's Allendale and Grand Rapids campuses.

  • Allendale: 8:00 AM-5:00 PM Mon-Sat
  • Pew/City Campus: 10:00 AM-8:00 PM Mon-Fri
